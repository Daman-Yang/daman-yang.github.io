## Overview
This is a school management and service platform for the secondary school, which has the interfaces of Weixin Mini-program and Website, implementing the functions below:
- Student forum and associations' notifications
- Campus service: Day-off application, dormitory moral scoring and querying, advice collection etc.
- Students management: Maintenance of students' data and campus-related data 

## Technical
**Full stack**
- SpringBoot
- Hibernate & JPA
- MySQL
- Vue.js
- Weixin Mini-program
