I regard myself as an **engineer-like and PM-like Product Engineer**, attentive to all aspects of building something from 0 to 1 -- from marketing, business and SCM to R&D and delivery etc. -- not just the technology. 

Look forward to any opportunities and communications about research and startup. Please feel free to reach out to me and engage in discussions on any topic.
